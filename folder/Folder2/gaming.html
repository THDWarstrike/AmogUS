What is Gaming

Gaming is something for our entertainment or fun. Each and every form of gaming works different from one another. The first Video Game was made late 1958 but games like baseball and Basketball have been around for thousands of years.

What are the different type of games

There are multiple types of games. Some of the most common ones are Role-Playing Games(RPG's), Video Games, card games, sports, and the good old fashioned board game. The oldest game out of all of these is the board games.
