History of Dungeons&Dragons

Description of the franchise

Dungeons&Dragons better known as D&D or DnD is a table-top role-playing game(rpg). Created by Gary Gygax and Dave Armeson in 1974. By 1997 the series was started to be released by Wizards of the Coast.

Advanced Dungeons&Dragons

Advanced Dungeons&Dragons or better known as AD&D is the first edition of the fantasy table-top rpg. Created in 1974 and published thru 1997 by TSR(Tactial Studio Rules). As the first edition of this now popular table-top rpg game, this has all the basic knowledge of how it was originally meant to be played. There are multiple books to this edition of the game and they are as listed - 1 players handbook, 1 Dunegons Master Guide(DMS Guide), 2 Monster Manuels, 1 Unearthed Arcana, 1 Deities and Demigods, and 1 Oriental Adventures

AD&D 2nd edition

This is the 2nd version of the Dungeons&Dragons series. This edition was released in 1989 and published by TSR. Some of the major differences between the two are the list of classes, the presumption of non-weapon proficiencies,Advancements of theif skills,nature of bards,Kits,Specialist Mages, Clerics, THACo,Psionics. This version of the fan favorite rpg game was an improvement from AD&D but still had it's problems.

D&D 3rd Edition

The third version of the D&D series, it was released in 2000 by Wizards of the Coast. This version is 3rd ranked in popularity acording to GameRant. It of course has improvements from 2nd edition. What changed in this version is that Wizards of The Coast removed the word Advanced from the title so the new players won't get confused, and it received a revision in 2003 and the revision is known as 3.5.

D&D 3.5

Acording to GameRant 3.5 is the 2nd most popular version of the game. There is not that much that changed in this versio from the previous version of 3rd edition. This version is just a revision of 3rd edition but with features that the D&D community loves.

D&D 4th Edition

This version is the most hated in the entire community ask any D&D player and they will tell you why the hate this version.

D&D 5th Edition

The most popular version of the game. Fifth Edition is also the most recent version of the rpg. The game released 7 years ago from today. It was released by Wizards of the Coast. For the features of this game it works just like it predecessor versions but it lets you warp the world you create to your liking.
